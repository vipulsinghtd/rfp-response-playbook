Account Executive
Responsibilities
01

Communicate RFP Receipt

Notify the Proposal Manager the moment an RFP arrives. No delays — the clock starts immediately.

02

Share Packet & Determine Format

Deliver the complete RFP to the PM. Determine if it's Q&A or open format. If open format, collaborate with the SE to decide the best response vehicle — slides, Word doc, or other — before handoff.

03

Set Clear Due Dates

Communicate the client-facing deadline and internal submission cutoff clearly and in writing.

04

Provide Support Resources

  • Complete MEDDPICC check before handoff to PM
  • Share HubSpot opportunity link with the Proposal Manager
  • Create a dedicated Slack channel for the RFP project

Proposal Manager — Intake
Loopio Intake Process
01

Run the Loopio Skill

Process the RFP through the Loopio answering skill to auto-generate initial responses.

02

Upload to Loopio

Import the skill output into Loopio with the correct project and section structure.

03

Assign Stakeholders

Tag SC / SA / SE reviewers per question. Every question must have a named owner.

04

Ensure 100% Coverage

Verify every question is uploaded — no sections skipped. Manual edits where needed.

Proposal Manager — QA & Export
Quality Check & Export
PM's Scope
Completeness,
not accuracy.
The PM verifies every question has been answered. Accuracy of the content is owned by the SE — the PM does not rewrite or validate answers.
Export
Send to required format
Export the completed RFP into the client's required template — Word, slides, or PDF.
☑️Every Question Answered
No blank answers. No skipped sections. Every question has a response before export.
☑️Full Coverage
All sections, sub-sections, and appendix questions accounted for — nothing dropped.
☑️Fonts & Formatting
Consistent fonts, spacing, and style throughout. No mixed formatting or off-template text.
☑️Images Included
All screenshots, diagrams, and media are placed correctly in the final document.

Solution Engineer / SC / SA
Responsibilities
01

Work Inside Loopio Only

All answering and reviewing happens exclusively inside Loopio. No parallel documents or offline edits — ever.

Answer & Review Questions

Answer questions the Loopio skill could not fill. Review and validate every AI-generated answer for accuracy.
